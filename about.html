About

Courabie offers the ultimate coffee and cookie experience. We hand-pick our gourmet coffees from responsibly-sourced, sustainably-grown Deeper Roots roasters (Cincinnati, OH) and pair them with our light, healthy homemade shortbread cookies and biscottis to guarantee a pleasant morning or dolce-far-niente afternoon.

We couple the finest coffee with a unique Greek cookies made by Irene's family that is more delightful than any other treat we've ever paired with coffee. We are proud to create a space that is welcoming, enjoyable and with sleek modern design.

The logo pattern includes two identical features of Courabie: Coffee and Greece cookies. They are bounded together with a line, this indicates that are often serve together.
